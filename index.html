Features

Everything a tired parent needs

Built around the real rhythm of a baby's day — not a rigid alarm clock, but a living plan that adapts as the day unfolds.

⚡
Instant schedule generation

Enter your baby's wake-up time and NapMap builds the entire day — awake windows, naps, and bedtime — in one tap.

🟢
Live status, always current

A real-time card tells you exactly what's happening right now — whether baby is awake, napping, or heading to bed — with a live countdown.

✏️
One-tap adjustments

Baby fell asleep early? Woke up late? Tap once to adjust any block and every subsequent time cascades automatically.

📅
Auto-reset every morning

Each new day starts fresh automatically. No manual clearing, no leftover data — just open the app and enter today's wake time.

🌙
Flexible nap templates

Configure your baby's awake window durations and nap lengths in settings. NapMap adapts as your baby grows and schedules evolve.

🌗
Dark & light mode

Designed for middle-of-the-night checks. A polished dark theme is the default, with a clean light mode for daytime use.

How it works

Up and running in 10 seconds

No account, no setup, no subscriptions. Just open it and go.

1
Enter wake-up time

Type in the time your baby woke up this morning — that's all the information NapMap needs.

2
Get the full day plan

NapMap generates an alternating awake–nap–awake schedule from wake-up through bedtime, based on your settings.

3
Follow along in real time

The live card tracks the current window and counts down to the next transition. Adjust any block with a tap if reality changes.

🔒
100% private, by design

NapMap has no backend, no accounts, and no analytics. Every piece of data you enter stays on your device only — and auto-clears every morning. We built it this way intentionally, because your family's routine is yours alone.
