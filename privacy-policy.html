Privacy Policy

Baby Sleep Planner  ·  Last updated: March 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Baby Sleep Planner ("the App", "we", "us") handles information when you use our mobile application. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information. The App operates entirely on your device. No data is transmitted to any server operated by us or any third party.

The only data the App stores is your baby's first name and daily sleep schedule settings (wake-up time, nap durations, awake window lengths). This information is saved exclusively in your device's local storage and never leaves your device.

2. Data Storage

All app data is stored locally on your device using browser/app local storage. You can delete this data at any time by using the "Reset to Defaults" option in the app's Settings screen, or by uninstalling the app.

3. Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising networks, crash-reporting services, or tracking SDKs. No information is shared with any third party.

4. Children's Privacy

The App is designed to assist parents and caregivers of infants. It does not collect any information from or about children. The baby's name you optionally enter is stored only on your own device and is never transmitted anywhere.

5. Permissions

The App does not request access to your camera, microphone, contacts, location, or any other device sensor or service.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted at this URL with an updated "Last updated" date. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
